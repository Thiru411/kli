BEWARE OF SPURIOUS / FRAUD PHONE CALLS
IRDAI is not involved in activities like selling insurance policies, announcing bonus or investment of premiums. Public receiving such phone calls are requested to lodge a police complaint. Beware of spurious emails with fictitious/fraudulent offers. Persons receiving such emails mentioning Kotak Life are requested to highlight the same immediately to kli.riskcontrol@kotak.com with "Spurious Email" in the subject line.
